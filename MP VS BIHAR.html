State Comparison: Madhya Pradesh vs Bihar

Madhya Pradesh

  • Cultural Heritage: Known as the "Heart of India", MP is rich in tribal traditions, classical dance forms like Rai and Matki, vibrant festivals like Bhagoria and Malwa Utsav, and historical architecture like Khajuraho temples and the Buddhist Sanchi Stupa.
  • Food: Famous dishes include Poha, Bhutte ka Kees, Dal Bafla, and Jalebi.
  • Literacy Rate: Around 70.6% (as of 2021).

Bihar

  • Cultural Heritage: Bihar is known for its ancient learning centers like Nalanda and Vikramshila, traditional folk arts like Madhubani painting, festivals like Chhath Puja and Sonepur Mela, and classical music and dance. Bodh Gaya is an important Buddhist pilgrimage site.
  • Food: Traditional dishes include Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, Thekua, and Khaja.
  • Literacy Rate: Around 63.8% (as of 2021).
